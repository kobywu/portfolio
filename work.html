My partner and I created a game where the knight has to save the princess by jumping or ducking over obstacles/animals. If you successfully jump over 20 obstacles, you saved the princess.

Circuit

We learned how to create a series, parallel circuit to light up 2 LED lights with a button. I learned that in a breadboard, the rows are electrically connected while columns aren't. Also that a red wire would be connected to the power source while the black wire connects to the ground. The taller LED side goes next to the resistor while the short LED side goes by the ground.
